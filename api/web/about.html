About NBA Stables

NBA Stables is a free web app built for basketball fans who want fast, real-time NBA stats without the clutter. No ads, no paywalls, no tracking — just clean data updated live during every game.

What You Get

🏀

Live Scoreboard

Real-time scores for every NBA game with quarter-by-quarter breakdowns and game status.

📊

Box Scores

Full team and player stats for today's games and the past week.

🔍

Player Tracker

Search any NBA player and track their live stats across multiple games at once.

🏆

Daily Leaders

See who's leading the league in points, rebounds, assists, steals and blocks each day.

📈

Standings

Up-to-date Eastern and Western Conference standings with win-loss records.

🩹

Injury Reports

Latest NBA injury updates so you always know who's in and who's out.

Why NBA Stables?

Most sports sites are bloated with ads, pop-ups and slow-loading pages. NBA Stables strips all of that away. It's a lightweight progressive web app (PWA) that works on any device — desktop, tablet or phone. You can even install it to your home screen for an app-like experience.

Always Free

NBA Stables is and will always be completely free. No account needed, no data collected. Just open the site and get your stats.
